Tic-Tac-Toe

Rules

The game is played on a 3x3 grid. Player X goes first. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner. If all 9 squares are full, the game is a draw.
